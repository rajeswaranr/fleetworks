Frequently Asked Questions

How does FleetWorks pricing work?

You get an estimated price instantly when you book. After physical inspection, the workshop shares a final itemised quote — work starts only after your approval. No hidden charges.

Do you provide doorstep service?

Yes. Battery replacement, tyre puncture, minor electrical work and inspections are done at your doorstep or roadside. Major mechanical jobs are done at partner workshops, with pickup assistance available.

What warranty do I get?

Up to 6 months / 20,000 km warranty on service labour, plus the manufacturer's warranty on all parts (batteries and tyres carry their brand warranty).

How fast is breakdown assistance?

Inside city limits we target under 90 minutes. On major highways (NH-44, NH-48, Golden Quadrilateral corridors) our partner network covers most stretches within 2 hours.

Can I get GST invoices for my company?

Absolutely. Every job comes with a proper GST invoice against your GSTIN, and fleet customers get consolidated monthly statements.
