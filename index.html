Frequently asked questions

How does pricing work?

You get an instant estimate when you book. After inspection, the workshop shares a final itemised quote — work starts only after your approval. Our low-price guarantee means we'll match a cheaper quote for the same genuine work nearby.

Are the parts genuine?

Yes. Every job uses genuine spares and high-grade oils & lubricants, with the bill to prove it. Every part replaced is logged to your vehicle's digital history — down to a single bolt.

What warranty do I get?

Workmanship and parts carry a guaranteed warranty as per terms & conditions — provided in writing with every job. Batteries and tyres also carry their brand warranty.

Is the fleet software really free to try?

Yes — a 15-day free trial with no card needed. You sign up with a GST number or PAN to verify your business. After the trial, keep your data and choose a plan that fits your fleet.

How fast is breakdown assistance?

Inside city limits we target under 90 minutes. On major highways our partner network covers most corridors within 2 hours.
