Partner FAQs

How much does it cost to join?

Nothing. Registration and onboarding are free. FleetWorks earns a commission (typically 10–15%) only on jobs we bring you — no job, no fee.

How do I receive jobs?

Through the FleetWorks Partner app. You get a job alert with vehicle details and issue description; accept it, share your quote, and once the customer approves, the job is yours.

When do I get paid?

Payouts hit your bank account within 48 hours of job completion and customer sign-off. Fleet AMC work is settled in weekly cycles.

What documents do I need?

PAN is mandatory. GSTIN, shop establishment licence and workshop photos are needed before go-live — our team helps you if any are missing.

Can mobile mechanics without a workshop join?

Yes. If you have your own tools and a two-wheeler or service van, you can join as a doorstep partner for batteries, tyres, electrical and minor mechanical jobs.
